Pho

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese soup consisting of broth, rice noodles, and meat, usually beef or chicken. It is a popular street food in Vietnam and has gained international popularity for its delicious and complex flavors.

  • Broth
  • Rice Noodles
  • Meat
